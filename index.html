Discover

Places to Explore

Tongatapu is full of stunning spots, all within easy scooter distance.

🌊

Mapu'a 'a Vaea Blowholes

Spectacular natural blowholes shooting water metres into the air. One of Tonga's most iconic sights.

🏖️

Ha'atafu Beach

Beautiful white sand beach perfect for swimming and snorkelling. A must-visit on the western coast.

🪨

Ha'amonga Trilithon

Tonga's ancient stone gate, often called the "Stonehenge of the Pacific". A fascinating piece of history.

🛍️

Talamahu Market

Nuku'alofa's vibrant market. Fresh tropical fruit, handmade crafts, and authentic Tongan food.
